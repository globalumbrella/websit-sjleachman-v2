A Bit About My Trading

Overview Analysis Stratagy Risk Managment

I'm knowledgeable in all asset classes but I have the most experience trading Forex and Option derivatives on U.S. Equities.

My approach to the market can be summed up into 3 section Analytics, Strategy & Risk Management. Through my consistent and disciplined approach I've been able to realize, on average a annual net gain of +90%. Farther more this has been achieved by maintaining no greater than a 5% drawdown.

I deploy a rigorous set of analytics to determine the logical future course of a asset. This includes the following:

  • levels of support and resistance
  • Fibonacci analysis
  • Price Momentum
  • Patterns
  • Breakouts
  • Reversals
  • Time Series Analysis
  • & A Variety Of Technical Indicators.

I construe a portfolio with a diverse set of multi leg option strategy's that on average are placed with 30 days until expiration. Execution of a strategy varies on the market condition e.g. Price, Time and Volatility. Some strategies that I currently implement within my portfolio are as fallowed:

  • Vertical Spread
  • Butterfly Spreads
  • Iron Candor
  • Calendar Spreads

Risk management Is the most important aspect of trading the fanatical market. This also holds true for any investment, nothing is gained without a level of risk. I manage risk through diversification in uncorrelated assets, and maintaining a market neutral portfolio.

This approach has allowed me to realize consistent returns within the market for around 4 years now. If you're interested in the markets, come check me out on Tradingview.com
